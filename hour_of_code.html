"While you're coding, you can have fun."


A project that illustrates that anyone can code is the Hour of Code. This project is made up of fun tutorials and operations that allow users to enjoy coding. During Computer Science Education Week, which is December 7-13, it is held annually. As ADA students, we arranged the event with the students of Secondary School No. 251 in Azerbaijan. Sadly, we had to keep the event online on December 11, due to the pandemic situation. 43 students participated successfully and completed the course, despite all constraints. Aygun Azizova who is the Director of Secondary School No. 251 helped us willinly and set up everything properly.
