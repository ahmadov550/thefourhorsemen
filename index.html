We are Team 01. The amazing movie "Now You See Me" inspired us for choosing "The Four Horsemen" as our team name. In the movie four street magicians (3 male and 1 female), Daniel, Merritt, Henley and Jack, ransack a huge amount of money belonging to insurance baron Arthur Tressler while being chased by FBI agent Dylan and Interpol agent Alma. We do not have anything to do with magic, but instead we are four programmers. Because of their magic they will always be remembered as legends. We aimed to achieve the same, but with our codes.
